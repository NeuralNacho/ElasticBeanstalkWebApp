Phantom

Othello Rules:

▼
  1. Black places the first disc.
  2. Place discs on squares where it surrounds a sequence of discs of the opposite colour at both ends. Blue highlighted squares show the possible moves.
  3. The surrounded tiles flip to the opposite colour.
  4. If a player has no move their turn passes.
  5. The game ends when both players have no moves and the winner is the player with the most tiles of their colour.
